895 by the Numbers

Breaking down Alex Ovechkin's record goal total

By Andy Parr

Alex Ovechkin scored his 895th career goal Sunday against the Islanders. The marker put him ahead of Wayne Gretzky for the NHL's all-time lead in goals. Here is a comparison of how each reached their totals.

Despite losing the all-time goals record to Ovechkin, Gretzky is still the all-time leader in both even strength and short handed goals. Ovechkin is the NHL's all-time leader in power play goals, a record he's held since 2022.
